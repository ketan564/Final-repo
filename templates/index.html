URL Safety Tips

  • Check for HTTPS in the URL (secure connection)
  • Verify domain spelling (watch for typos like "amaz0n.com")
  • Avoid clicking on shortened URLs (bit.ly, tinyurl, etc.)

Warning Signs

  • Suspicious domain names (e.g., paypal-secure.otherdomain.com)
  • URLs with excessive numbers or special characters
  • HTTP instead of HTTPS for sites requiring login credentials

Email Security Tips

  • Verify the sender's email address carefully
  • Be suspicious of unexpected attachments
  • Hover over links before clicking to see the actual URL

Warning Signs

  • Urgent requests or threatening language
  • Poor grammar, spelling, or formatting
  • Requests for sensitive information or credentials
